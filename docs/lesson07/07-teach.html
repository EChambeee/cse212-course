CSE 212 | Programming with Data Structures

07 Teach: Group Collaboration

Instructions

As a group, spend one hour collaborating together on the 06-Prove problems. You must not share your actual code solutions with each other. During this time together, you should share strategy, technique, and examples to help each other learn the material. Make sure that each person in your group has an opportunity to share ideas and ask questions. You should prepare yourself for this activity by starting and completing several of the problems from the individual assignment.
